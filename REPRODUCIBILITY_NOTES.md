# Reproducibility and interpretation notes

## Final numbering

The repository uses only the final journal numbering. `FINAL_NUMBERING_MAP.tsv` documents the temporary numbering used during revision and its final mapping.

## Journal workbooks and deposited source sheets

The journal submission uses `Table_S1.xlsx`-`Table_S14.xlsx`. To keep the public repository reviewable and portable, the repository deposits the journal-numbered TSV source sheets rather than duplicating the binary workbooks. `08_write_supplementary_tables.R` rebuilds the Excel workbooks from those source sheets. Table S8 additionally requires the region-level output generated by `03B_threshold_and_continuous_sensitivity.R`. The manually curated published-evidence synthesis underlying Table S14 is deposited as a cited TSV source sheet.

## Two different native-versus-designed motif summaries

The manuscript reports two related but distinct scans:

1. The broad motif-database scan underlying Figs. 1-2 and Tables S1-S3 reports 8 AP2/ERF motif-position hits in the native sequence and 45 in the designed sequence.
2. The separately validated family-focused constrained-mutant scan underlying Fig. S1/Table S4 reports 9 and 61, respectively.

These values use different scoped analyses and must not be substituted for one another.

## HSF redundancy

Raw HSF counts are motif-model/position matches. Related HSF PWMs can recognize the same overlapping interval. `04_nonredundant_HSF_locations.R`, Fig. S2 and Table S6 therefore report merged non-redundant HSF-compatible locations separately.

## Threshold and continuous-score analysis

`03B_threshold_and_continuous_sensitivity.R` evaluates thresholds 0.80, 0.85, 0.90 and 0.95 and three threshold-independent regional metrics. Final exact summary values are deposited in `source_data/S8A_threshold_stats.tsv`, `source_data/S8B_continuous_stats.tsv` and `source_data/S8D_QC.tsv`; the full region-level sheet is regenerated by the workflow.

## Figure 7 logo models

The final Fig. 7D representative models are:

- HSFC1 — JASPAR **MA1667.2**
- DOF1.8 — JASPAR **MA0981.2**

Their PFMs and metadata are deposited under `motifs/` and `source_data/Figure7_logo_model_metadata.csv`. The logos describe PWM preferences and do not demonstrate occupancy or regulatory function in vivo.

## Figure assembly

The analysis scripts generate panel source files and tabular results. Journal-ready assembled figures are submission assets and are not duplicated in this code/data repository. `REPRODUCIBILITY_MATRIX.tsv` maps every final display item to its responsible script, input and generated panel output. Manual panel assembly and spacing do not alter plotted values.

## Methylation and RNA-seq limitations

The methylome is from unstressed Col-0 leaves and is used as basal chromatin context. The RNA-seq experiment used whole seedlings and is not paired to that methylome. Candidate-window RNA-seq signal uses fractional multimapping-aware counting and is not interpreted as definitive copy-specific expression.

## Accession ascertainment

Accession candidates were recovered with a Col-0-derived 49-bp seed allowing up to four mismatches. Similarity and motif-retention statements therefore apply to the detected candidate set and do not establish conservation across all ONSEN-related sequences.
